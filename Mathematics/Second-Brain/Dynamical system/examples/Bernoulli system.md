## Thm :: Ornstein
- $X=\prod_{i\in\mathbb{Z}}\{0,...,k\}$ with a Bernoulli measure, shift $T:X\rightarrow X$;
- $Y=\prod_{i\in\mathbb{Z}}\{0,...,l\}$ with a Bernoulie measure, shift $S:Y\rightarrow Y$.
Then $T$ and $S$ are isomorphic if and only if $h(T)=h(S)$.
